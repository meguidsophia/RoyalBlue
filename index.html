About this Website

On this website, you first can read "The Very Hungry Caterpillar" by Eric Carle (posted below) and can then play a few minigames related to the book. If you click on the items activities in the navigation bar, you can create a Mad Lib, take a comprehension quiz, and animate a character from the book.

The Very Hungry Caterpillar is a children's picture book designed, illustrated, and written by Eric Carle, first published by the World Publishing Company in 1969, later published by Penguin Putnam.
